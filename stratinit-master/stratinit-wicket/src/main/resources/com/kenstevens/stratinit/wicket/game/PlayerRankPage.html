Leader Board

Player Rank is the weighted average of a player's Team Ranks. Ranks are weighted by the number of opponent teams defeated by each team you played on. To be precise, Player Rank = sum((team rank) * (team victories)) / (total team victories).

The original Leader Board is still available, although I consider the Elo ranking to be a better measurement of player skill as it takes into consideration the skill level of the players you beat.
